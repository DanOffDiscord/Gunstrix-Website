About Me


What's up guys! My name is Gunstrix (or Bradley), whatever you prefer! Thank you so much for dropping by and checking my website out! I am 17 years old, from the UK (Bracknell). I started streaming on June 2, 2018. If you watch even a little bit of my streams you will realize that I love to have fun, stay positive, and enjoy myself to the greatest of my ability! I also LOVE interacting with you guys in the chat while I stream and the comments when I post a video! I like to stream mostly shooter games like Fortnite and Call of Duty but I do sometimes upload a video of Roblox or Rocket League.

I appreciate that you took the time to read this and try to get to know me. If you have any questions or concerns, please just let me know in chat or the comments. I am always looking to get to know and interact with you guys, don't be shy!
